# eda_real_estate_pricing
Dynamics of House Valuation in a Dynamic Market

In the dynamic landscape of the residential real estate market, determining an optimal and
competitive price for a house is a multifaceted challenge. As a key member of the analytics team
in a leading real estate company, your task is to conduct a comprehensive analysis to identify and
understand the myriad variables that significantly influence house prices. By leveraging
advanced data analytics techniques and visualization tools, your goal is to uncover patterns,
correlations, and trends within the dataset, enabling the company to make informed decisions and
strategically position properties for better business opportunities.
